About Us


A little about me

I build and show custom LED art installations among several other creations in various mediums. 3D printing has opened up Pandora's box and allowed me to get things out of my head and into reality. I have a background in working with my hands and I love seeing physical progress of an idea coming ot life. I'm a life long tinkerer and got started with programming after playing with electronics for awhile and then wondering how do people get their electronic projects to listen to their commands?!


Software development is a next step for me to change the world and create fun and useful tools. I'm excited to get my creativity onto millions of screens and make life just a little bit easier and fun.
